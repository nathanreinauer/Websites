Childlife Montessori has 30 children ages 3 to 6. In an environment of mutual respect, the design addresses all areas of learning within the Montessori philosophy: Sensorial, Practical, Mathematics, Cultural & Language, and Reading. The classroom allows the children a seamless access to each while promoting independence and creativity through flexibility and adaptability, while allowing adults 360 degree sightlines for supervising.
