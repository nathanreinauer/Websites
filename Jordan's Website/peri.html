The clients kitchen was spacious yet dysfunctional, and their powder bath was lacking identity. Both of the clients loved to garden and cook with their fresh ingredients so they needed a kitchen space planned for not just one, but two constant cooks. They wanted the kitchen to have a Moroccan aesthetic. By adding additional storage, a new pantry, an access door to the garden, and ample prep/cooking space, the clients got a kitchen that provides everything they need, with the aesthetic that they love.
