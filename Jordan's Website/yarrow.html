Yarrow is a women’s health and fertility clinic located on the 3rd floor of a building in downtown Portland. As “the flower of good health,” Yarrow’s goal was to bring natural elements into a typically sterile and uncomfortable environment. Through evidence based design, all selections of finishes, artwork, and all surroundings were chosen to put the patient’s mind at ease. The clinic became a space of comfort and healing during a stressful and emotional time for the patients. My scope of work in this collaboration included: full space planning, technical drawings & details, graphic layout, FF&E + finish selection.
