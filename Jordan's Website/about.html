ABOUT JORDAN
Hi! My name is Jordan Kline and I am currently a student at The Art Institute of Portland studying Interior Design and Sustainability. As an honors student, I’ve taken on tutoring at The Art Institute – tutoring AutoCad, Revit, SketchUp, Adobe Suites, and more. I tutor in-class as an assistant to instructors, as well as one-on-one tutoring appointments. I am fortunate enough to have worked not one, but two internships before completing my bachelor’s degree. I have also done freelance work for a general contractor. While gaining professional experience in both healthcare and hospitality, I have built a great foundation to start my career as an interior designer.
