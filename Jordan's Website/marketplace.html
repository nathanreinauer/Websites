The northern end of the Pearl District of Portland, Oregon is rapidly growing as it becomes one of the most popular relocation destinations in the country. With only three grocery stores to support a daytime population of nearly 120,000 people, The Marketplace fills a niche in the neighborhood, providing a shopping experience unlike any other in the Pearl. With a flower bar, a wine bar, and European market, The Marketplace can be a quick stop for ingredients on your way home from work, or a weekend outing with friends.
